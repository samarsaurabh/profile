About Me

Tarun Kumar Banerjee has done his M.Sc. from Kolkata University and PGDCA from Punjab University.

He belongs to Hooghly District. Of West Bengal .He is an educator, management consultant, senior corporate trainer, motivational speaker, and a first generation entrepreneur. He is associated with various International and National Organizations of Govt. of India ,Govt of HP, Punjab, Haryana, Various Universities ,IIT ,schools and colleges and also member of various professional organizations . He has conducted training programs for more than one hundred organizations namely L & T ,Godrej, Hero group,Vardhaman, Empire Packaging, Maruti ,Bata, Colgate,Indian oil, National Productivity council, Nittttr, Ministry of finance, Ministry of Environment and Forest, CRRID,RICM,VERKA,BBMB,KRIBHCO,CSIO,ISTC,NIPER,MAGSIPA,HIPA,SJVN,Jan siksha sansthan,Ark industries ,Mahindra and Mahindra etc He is empanelled as a corporate trainer for Panjab university. kolkata university, Punjab enginering college ,Chandigarh college of engineering and technology, Swami Vivekanand college, Sachdeva group of colleges. He was instrumental in various MOUs between academic institutions and Corporates. He participates in curriculum development workshops in Academic institutions like NIELIT ,NCERT etc. He has published various papers and articles in Journals and magazines. On the lighter side he has also played Badminton at National level.
